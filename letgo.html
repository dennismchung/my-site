Letgo

Letgo is a marketplace app that focuses on getting users to buy and sell items quickly.

Goal

Letgo is easy to use and allows users to sell their belongings with just a few taps on their phone. Another unique value Letgo brings to the table is ther ML/AI camera technology that automatically detects an object in it's camera feature. After identifing the item using it's database it gives the average time it takes to sell and price.

My role as a product designer I did usability tests, user interviews, user research, Hifi prototyping and testing to make Letgo's experience more enjoyable and trustworthy.
