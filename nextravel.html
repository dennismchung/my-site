Nextravel

Travel service that provides companies a way to offer Flight, car rental and hotel services to their employees on one platform.

Goal

Over the last 3 years the NexTravel product has grown organically based on customer requests and feedback. Our team was tasked to redesign the information architecture and site navigation.

My role as a product designer for our client involved User interviews, Usability Testing and Card Sorting to better understand the user archetypes for Nextravel. As a team member I designed sketched, and made Lofi/Hifi prototypes for responsive web. I also conducted User testing and iterated off our results to make a more efficient Information Architecture and usable interace.

Results:

  • 8/10 Both Admin and non-Admin users were able to navigate the website using icons only for the top navigation.
  • 10/10 users thought that the new icon layout were more intuitive than the existing icons
  • 8/10 users found that the newly designed menu was easier to navigate to achieve their goal than the previous navigation.
